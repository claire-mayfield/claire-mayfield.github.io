About Claire

Passing Time, a composition by Claire Mayfield





Claire Mayfield is a composer, sound designer, and developer for video games currently studying in Boston, MA. At the Berklee College of Music on a World Tour Scholarship, she is double majoring in Game & Interactive Media Scoring and Film & Media Scoring and minoring in Conducting. Claire has placed on the Dean's List for every semester she has attended. Throughout her studies at Berklee, Claire has gained leadership experience as the concertmaster and PR Manager for the Audire Soundtrack Collective, as a concertmaster for the Berklee World Strings, and as an assistant concertmaster for the Berklee Contemporary Symphony Orchestra and the Berklee Motion Picture Orchestra.

Claire's compositions have been played in several concerts by the Berklee World Strings, and she has also arranged video game soundtracks for the Audire Soundtrack Collective. Claire also works as a session musician, recording violin in student compositions for films, TV, and video games. Claire is the sound designer for the video game Lost in an Illusion, as part of a collaboration project with USC students. She also has experience with both performing and directing foley sessions, as well as directing voice acting.
